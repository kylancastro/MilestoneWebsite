Milestone 1: First Generation of Headphones

The first generation of headphones couldn't even be put on your head; they were handheld items.

Key features included:

  • Large and bulky design
  • Limited sound quality
  • Used primarily in professional settings

The first generation of headphones was primarily used in professional settings, offering limited sound quality and a bulky design.
