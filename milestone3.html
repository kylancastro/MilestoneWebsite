Milestone 3: Third Generation of Headphones

The third generation of headphones aimed to reduce size and improve convenience. They became more portable and user-friendly.

Key features included:

  • Compact design for easy transport
  • Enhanced comfort with padded ear cups
  • Better sound quality with advanced technology

The third generation headphones were designed for portability and comfort, making them ideal for everyday use.
