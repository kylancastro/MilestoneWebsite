Milestone 2: Second Generation of Headphones

The second generation of headphones was characterized by their bulky design. They were much larger than the first generation and were often used in professional settings.

Key features included:

  • Over-ear design for better sound isolation
  • Improved sound quality
  • Heavyweight materials

The second generation headphones provided better sound isolation and were designed for professional use.
