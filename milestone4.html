Milestone 4: Fourth Generation of Headphones

The fourth generation of headphones marked the transition to wireless technology, paving the way for Bluetooth headphones.

Key features included:

  • Wireless connectivity for greater freedom
  • Longer battery life
  • Improved sound quality with digital audio processing

The fourth generation headphones introduced wireless technology, allowing users to enjoy music without the hassle of cords.
